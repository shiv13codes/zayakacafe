About Us

ZAYAKA Cafe is not just a place to eat; it’s an experience. Inspired by eclectic and industrial designs, our café is adorned with unique mechanical artwork, superhero masks, and props that create a surreal atmosphere. Established to bring people together through great food, music, and ambiance, ZAYAKA Cafe offers an unforgettable experience, whether you're here for a quiet coffee or an electrifying DJ night.

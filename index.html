The Story

ZAKAYA is more than just a café — it's an experience.

Founded with a vision to create a space that's both industrial and magical, ZAKAYA Cafe is adorned with mechanical artwork, superhero masks, and other unique props that create an unforgettable atmosphere.

Whether you're looking for a place to enjoy a quiet cup of coffee, meet friends for delicious food, or experience our vibrant evening events, ZAKAYA Cafe offers a setting unlike any other.
